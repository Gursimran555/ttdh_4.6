Why Most E-commerce Stores Underperform

The average e-commerce conversion rate is only 1–3%. That means 97–99% of your website visitors leave without buying. This isn't a traffic problem — it's a conversion problem. And it's almost always caused by the same underlying issues: slow loading times, poor mobile experience, confusing navigation, insufficient trust signals, and checkout friction.

TTDH builds e-commerce stores from the ground up with conversion optimisation embedded into every decision. Product page layouts designed to sell, checkout flows engineered to reduce abandonment, trust signals strategically placed throughout the journey, and performance optimisation that keeps Google happy and customers engaged.

Whether you're launching your first online store or migrating from a platform that's holding you back, we build solutions that are fast, scalable, SEO-friendly and designed to grow with your business for years to come.
