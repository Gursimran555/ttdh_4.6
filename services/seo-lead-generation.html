The Truth About SEO Most Agencies Won't Tell You

Most SEO agencies charge monthly retainers and deliver vague reports about "improvements" that never translate to actual revenue growth. They optimise for vanity metrics — keyword rankings that don't convert — and make themselves indispensable by creating complexity rather than delivering clarity.

At TTDH, we take a fundamentally different approach. We start by identifying the exact keywords your ideal customers use when they're ready to buy — high-intent, transactional search terms that drive real enquiries. We then build the technical, on-page and off-page SEO infrastructure needed to rank and convert for those terms. Every action we take is mapped to revenue, not rankings.

Combined with our lead generation systems — automated funnels, paid search management and conversion rate optimisation — we create a complete, predictable pipeline of qualified new business for your company every single month.
